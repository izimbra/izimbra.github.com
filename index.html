I Started a Blog

Date: 21 Jan 2013
Music: Low - I Started a Joke

What and Why

I wanted to start blogging for some time now, and here comes my first shot at it. The primary motivation is the need to share knowledge, which I see as a step in my quest for becoming a software craftsman.

I need to share what I learn for 2 simple reasons:

  • in order not to forget it
  • trying to explain things to others shows there's more to be learned.
    So basically this is going to be a programming blog, because this is what I'm really interested in and trying to do for a living. At the same time, I feel that my skills are really underdeveloped, especially when I read about young hackers creating fantastic software. I'm not that young (programming is my second career) and way far from daring to put the word hacker anywhere near me, but why not help this by exposing my ignorance?

Besides this main reason - I want to be a great programmer - I also want to be a more fluent, faster and better-styled writer (in that order). And how do I get to Carnegie Hall?

I will try writing texts here in the way I myself expect from a good technical reading:

  • to the point but with enough detail
  • with relevant references and good layout
  • and a bit of humour sometimes.

I'm not sure if I will put any personal information here, but at least I'm gonna say that my nickname is the anagram of my full name (the first being Eugene). I picked that up from another guy's programming blog :)
